WT_4_365 - UV irradiation (365 nm)

This unit was WaveyTech's first and most successful unit. Designed primarily for the irradiation of diazirine moeities, although it has been successfully implemented in photo-switching applications to. Any application which requires powerful irradiation at 365 nm, then this is the unit for you!

This unit contains four powerful UV LEDs (365 nm) for simultaneous sample exposure. - It is highly portable - It is safe, with design to ensure no stray irradation escapes the unit during operation - LEDs are supported by a heat quality alumminium housing and heatsinks to draw what little heat is generated away from your samples. Keeping them fresher for longer compared to irradiators using bulbs. - Fast irradiation times.

WT_4_457 - Blue irradiation (457 nm)

This unit is the newest addition to WaveyTech's product list. Designed primarily for the irradiation of diazirine moeities, using the emerging micro-map technology.

All the benefits and features of the UV unit - but with a new wavelength. This wavelength was also highly applicable to the photo-switching reactions, utilised in this peer-reviewed research completed by the Tate Research Group.
