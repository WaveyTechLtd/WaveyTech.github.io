WT_4_365 - UV irradiation (365 nm)

This unit was WaveyTech's first and most successful unit. Designed primarily for the irradiation of diazirine moeities, although it has been successfully implemented in photo-switching applications to. Any application which requires powerful irradiation at 365 nm, then this is the unit for you!

This unit contains four powerful UV LEDs (365 nm) for simultaneous sample exposure.

  • It is highly portable
  • It is safe, with design to ensure no stray irradation escapes the unit during operation
  • LEDs are supported by a heat quality alumminium housing and heatsinks to draw what little heat is generated away from your samples. Keeping them fresher for longer compared to irradiators using bulbs.
  • Fast irradiation times.

About Us

Dr. Charlie Saunders, got his PhD at Imperial College London under the supervision of Prof. Ed Tate.

In 2021, he started WaveyTech Ltd as a formal company and began work to achieve CE certification for these devices. So far WaveyTech has supplied units, academic and industrial research institutions in the UK and wider Europe.
